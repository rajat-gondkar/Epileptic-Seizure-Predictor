EEG Signal Preprocessing Pipeline

CHB-MIT Scalp EEG Database — Patient chb01 · chb01_03.edf

Preprocessing Pipeline Steps

1

Channel Selection

Select 18 standard 10-20 bipolar channels, deduplicate to 17 unique channels.

2

Bandpass Filter

4th-order Butterworth: 0.5 – 70 Hz. Removes DC drift and high-frequency noise.

3

Notch Filter

Remove 60 Hz power line interference (US standard).

4

Artifact Rejection

Reject epochs where amplitude exceeds ±500 µV threshold.

5

Epoch Creation

5-second windows with 1-second stride. Preictal labels from 30-min seizure onset window.

6

Feature Extraction

13 features × 17 channels = 221 dimensions. Band powers, Hjorth, spike rate, entropy.

Genetic Feature Engineering

12-dimensional genetic vector per patient: 9 binary mutation flags (ClinVar), 2 pLI scores (gnomAD), 1 Polygenic Risk Score (GWAS).

Mutation Flags (ClinVar)

9 epilepsy-associated ion channel genes screened for pathogenic variants. Simulated using population carrier frequencies.

Constraint Scores & PRS

pLI scores from gnomAD measure gene intolerance to loss-of-function. PRS computed from 15 GWAS epilepsy SNPs.

CTGAN Pipeline

1

Summary Aggregation

Per-EDF file: mean & std of 13 features → 26 EEG + 12 genetic + 3 meta = 41 columns.

2

Log-Transform

Band powers (~10⁻¹⁰) → log₁₀(x + ε). Prevents precision loss.

3

StandardScaler

Z-score normalisation. Inverse-transformed after generation.

4

CTGAN Training

300 epochs, dim (256,256), 10 discrete columns.

5

Constraints

Biological rules: mutations ∈ {0,1}, pLI frozen, PRS clipped.
