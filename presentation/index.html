Preprocessing Pipeline Steps

1

Channel Selection

Select 18 standard 10-20 bipolar channels, deduplicate to 17 unique channels.

2

Bandpass Filter

4th-order Butterworth filter: 0.5 – 70 Hz. Removes DC drift and high-frequency noise.

3

Notch Filter

Remove 60 Hz power line interference (US standard).

4

Artifact Rejection

Reject epochs where amplitude exceeds ±500 µV threshold.

5

Epoch Creation

5-second windows with 1-second stride. Labels: interictal (0) or preictal (1) based on 30-min seizure onset window.

6

Feature Extraction

13 features × 17 channels = 221 dimensions per epoch. Band powers, Hjorth parameters, spike rate, sample entropy.

Genetic Feature Engineering

12-dimensional genetic vector per patient: 9 binary mutation flags (ClinVar), 2 pLI scores (gnomAD), 1 Polygenic Risk Score (GWAS).

Mutation Flags (ClinVar)

9 epilepsy-associated ion channel genes screened for pathogenic variants. Simulated using population carrier frequencies.

Constraint Scores & PRS

pLI scores from gnomAD measure gene intolerance to loss-of-function. PRS computed from 15 GWAS epilepsy SNPs.

CTGAN Synthetic Data Generation

Conditional Tabular GAN — 190 real → 1,000 synthetic records

CTGAN Pipeline

1

Summary Aggregation

Per-EDF file summary: mean & std of 13 features (averaged over 17 channels) → 26 EEG columns + 12 genetic + 3 meta = 41 features per file.

2

Log-Transform

Band powers (~10⁻¹⁰ V²/Hz) → log₁₀(x + ε) to prevent precision loss. Without this, CTGAN sees all band powers as zero.

3

StandardScaler

Z-score normalisation on continuous columns before CTGAN training. Inverse-transformed after generation.

4

CTGAN Training

300 epochs, generator/discriminator dim (256,256), batch size 500. 10 discrete columns (mutations + seizure label).

5

Generation + Constraints

1,000 synthetic records generated. Biological constraints enforced: mutation flags ∈ {0,1}, pLI frozen, PRS clipped, band powers ≥ 0.
